Tangletown Coffee Roasters

We love all things coffee!

Locally sourced fair trade coffee beans roasted to perfection in the heart of Seattle's Green Lake's Tangletown neighborhood.


Our drum roaster


We utilize a state of the art commercial drum roaster the not only produces excellent results, but also offers complete control and flexibility of the drum. In capable hands, drum roasters are capable of being configured to produce well developed light roasts characteristic of the best air roasters as well as being configured on the very next roast for dark, sweet, and syrupy Italian roasts. Simply put, drum roasters can do what the others can do and drum roasters can do what the others can't.

Our roasting process


Long before its brewed, Tangletown Coffee Roasters starts with top-quality beans harvested from the world's greatest estates. Our state of the art roasting plant utilizes a proprietary system using custom-designed pneumatic tubes to produce the freshest cup of coffee on the market today. When utilizing some of the finest beans available, and best practices, you can expect a finished, hand crafted product that's second is lovingly produced, packaged and shipped to consumers all over the world.

Our roasting facility


Our patented roasting plant first holds "green" beans in a series of clear storage columns. When it's time for roasting, the beans are automatically measured out by weight and then "whooshed" into the roaster via bursts of air through clear pneumatic tubes. It's exciting to watch, but the process isn't just for show: Those curved tubes are specifiavlly designed to keep the freshly roasted beans from breaking in transit. They are then dispensed into reatail bags at the peak of freshness.

John Doe, Owner

Community is the fuel that drives Tangletown Coffee. Family Direct Trade revolves around building both a personal and economic relationship with the coffee farmer and his family. Based on a long-term and ongoing sustainability, this relationship translates to the quality of service and product on the farm and at home


Coffee and passion alone supports our farmers. By producing high-quality crop yields, their coffee sells well above the commodities per pound.


Together, we ensure that the pursuit of excellence is well-rewarded. We strive to build relationships that last a lifetime. The result: our farmers enjoy a sustainable way of life, and we have access to their incredible coffees.

Tangletown Coffee Roaster History

Tangeltown Coffee Roaster opened its doors in August of 2000 in Seattle's Greenlake neighborhood. Starting off small, founder John Doe roasted right out of his Greenlake coffee shop, sharing his passion directly with our local neighborhood customers. Since then he's managed to grow his business and offer incredible coffee throughout the country, partnering with some of the best coffee farmers in the world.


With an emphasis on quality and relationships, Tangletown Coffee has established itself in some of Seattle's most dynamic neighborhoods. Our location fosters a strong sense of community and respect for coffee. We offer a welcome environment and great place to sample some of Seattle's most exceptional beans.

Jane Doe, Master Coffee Roaster

Even a good bean can end up bad. Though well-processed and lovingly grown, a coffee bean depends on the roaster and the roasting process to help it shine. If roasted too dark, the sugars and acidic qualities will be burned to a crisp, leaving behind an oily, flavorless wisp of former granduer. If roasted light, coffee will taste tangy and sour.


Each coffee is sample roasted and cupped multiple times before being sent to our production roaster. This allows an opportunity to experiment with the unroasted coffee and find the perfect temperature and time by which to bring out all of the delicious flavors. The process requires total attention and a strong knowledge of the coffee being used.
